The Predictor

What it is How it works How to get it

What it is

Predictor is a simple hand-held device with just two elements - an LED light and a button.

How it works

The device's light switches on exactly one second BEFORE the button is pushed.

The scientific principle on which the Predictor works is explained in the video below.

Basically, what happens is the device sends an electron one second back in time.
